Cosmopolitan Cocktail

Description

A cosmopolitan, or simply "cosmo," is a vodka cocktail made with triple sec, cranberry juice, and lime juice.
The drink was invented in the 1930s, but rose in popularity in the 1990s.
It became even more fashionable after it was frequently featured on Sex and the City.

Ingredients:

  • Vodka
  • Lime Juice
  • Triple Sec
  • Cranberry Juice

How to make a Cosmopolitan Cocktail?

  1. Combine vodka, lime juice, triple sec, and cranberry juice in a cocktail shaker with ice.

  2. Cover, shake, and strain into a cosmopolitan or martini glass.

  3. Traditionally, cosmopolitans are garnished with a twist of lime or lemon peel.

  4. For extra pizzazz, try garnishing with a Candied Lemon Peel or skewered fruits.
